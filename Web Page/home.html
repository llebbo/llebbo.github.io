Welcome

Welcome to Video Games Central. We cover a vast array of video games, consoles, and accessories. We don't just cover the current games, consoles, and accessories, but we also cover those in the past and in the near future. We want this to be a site that gamers come to on a daily basis to get the new updates on their favorite games. We also provide reviews on classic, new, and upcoming games, consoles, and accessories. This can help gamers decide if they want to purchase a game.
